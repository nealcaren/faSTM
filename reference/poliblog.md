# CMU 2008 Political Blog Corpus (poliblog5k)

5,000 political blog posts from the 2008 U.S. election (the stm vignette
example) as a ready-to-use `faSTM_corpus`. Metadata: `rating`
(Conservative/Liberal), `day` (1-365), `blog`, `text`.

## Usage

``` r
data(poliblog)
```

## Format

A `faSTM_corpus`: 5,000 documents, 2,632-term vocabulary.

## Source

Eisenstein & Xing (2010), via the stm package.
